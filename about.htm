OUR HISTORY

Where it all started

Travel Booker started one day when the owner, Carl, was looking for a trip to take his family on. He visited many sites and stores but only found what he wanted by calling the company’s in the specific country directly, he thought there has to be a better way and that’s where his idea came about. From gaining different and private trips to the big external company’s travel booker is able to offer trips for all sizes and styles. It then developed as the need for more requirements for a trip grew and Carl noticed that there was no group packages on the market for what he desired. He then created the all in one package which is what we offer today to make a holiday easy and simply process for you.
